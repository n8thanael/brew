mission

IMPROVE AS TEAMMATES &
HAVE FUN CAUSING MAYHEM

We prize cooperation, positivity, and patience. We know we're in the lower bracket and understand these 3 attributes are necessary at this stage of the game. To achieve our team mission our team policies are:

A:) ONE  Mandatory practice per-week. A second volunteer practice will be scheduled but is not required. On weeks when a competative match is scheduled, there will not be a mandatory practice.

B:) Complaints, "Schooling" and "Advice" directed towards individual players about builds, strategy, play styles etc. are certainly welcome WITH RESPECT. They will be funneled through direct message to N8THANAEL the team captain after every match. Frustrations during play are common, but are more productive for the wholeteam if handled with patience after games or before practices.
Game-Time is Fun-Time.

C:) It is more important in our minds to find the best personalities that can succeed together as a team. After this is achieved then we hope to find and practice the most effective strategies together as a team.

D:) Given we are in Bronze/Silver/Gold we feel "coms" and coordination are more valuable to winning a game than hard-setting roles or drafting meta-only heroes. Therefore your cooperation, positivity, and patience are more valuable to the team than your win-rate, hero-pool and individual skill level.

E:) "Try-hards" and "know-it-alls" simply need to find a different team.
